What's going on here?

Making public-domain information accessible

The majority of patents are never commercially exploited or licensed - this site provides a way to browse recently expired patents to find hidden gems and get inspired by existing inventions. The site uses the recently released USPTO-API to fetch newly expired patents each day. Check out the FAQ to learn more!
